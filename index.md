## Introduction

Leap & Know ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Symmetry Studio: Mandalas ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

**Our Promise:** We respect your privacy. We do not sell your data, and we only collect the minimum information necessary to improve your experience.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

**Mandala Artwork**
- The App allows you to create and save mandala artwork on your device
- All artwork is stored locally on your device using Core Data
- We do not have access to your artwork
- You control when and where to share your creations

**Photo Library Access**
- When you choose to save artwork to your Photos, we request permission
- We only access Photos to save your artwork‚Äînothing else
- You can revoke this permission in iOS Settings at any time
- We do not upload or access any other photos on your device

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Analytics Data (via Firebase/Google Analytics)**

To understand how the App is used and to improve your experience, we collect anonymous usage data:

- **App usage statistics:** Which features you use, how often, and for how long
- **Technical information:** Device type, iOS version, app version
- **Crash reports:** Error logs and stack traces to fix bugs
- **Performance data:** App loading times, response times

**What we DO collect:**
- Tool usage (pencil, brush, eraser, etc.)
- Pattern selection frequency
- Symmetry level preferences
- Export actions (share, save, copy)
- Session duration and frequency
- Feature adoption rates

**What we DO NOT collect:**
- Your name or email address
- Your artwork or creations
- Photos from your library
- Contacts or personal information
- Location data
- Payment information (handled by Apple)

**Anonymous Identifiers:**
- Firebase assigns a random anonymous ID to your device
- This ID cannot be used to identify you personally
- It helps us understand aggregate usage patterns

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Deletion

**Your Artwork:**
- Stored only on your device
- You can delete individual artworks in the Gallery
- Delete the App to remove all local data

**Analytics Data:**
- Anonymous and cannot be linked to you
- You can opt-out using iOS settings (see below)

### How to Opt-Out of Analytics

**iOS 14.5 and later:**
1. Open iOS **Settings**
2. Go to **Privacy** ‚Üí **Tracking**
3. Toggle off **Allow Apps to Request to Track**
4. Or disable tracking for Symmetry Studio specifically

**Alternatively:**
1. Open iOS **Settings**
2. Scroll down to **Symmetry Studio**
3. Toggle off analytics (if option available)

### Revoking Photo Library Permission

1. Open iOS **Settings**
2. Scroll down to **Symmetry Studio**
3. Tap **Photos**
4. Select **None** or **Selected Photos**
